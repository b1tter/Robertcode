The story of one of the best stand up comedians in Denmark!

His name is Mahamad Habane but his stage name is Habane. He is 26 years old and lives in Aarhus. After high school he wanted to study medicine, but instead he opened his eyes for Stand-up. Habane spent a long time writing jokes, with the intension that later on it would end up being for stand-up shows. He has spent a long time practicing in front of the mirror and then test material on friends and acquaintances to see if the material he had could be put together for a show. After long time of practicing he had to also find the courage to appear in public forum before an audience he had never seen before. He has spent much of his time to strengthen himself and his appearance on the V58, which is a place in Aarhus, where you can go and test your jokes in front of an unknown audience for free. When he eventually got better, he started to make comedy as a “full-time” hobby beside his actual work. He booked himself into shows, which was free of charge both for him but also for his audience. Over time he began to do volunteer stand-up and still do as an exercise. Habane says that there is very few that can live on doing stand-up, but if he got the opportunity later in life he would with no doubt do it.
